Travelling to Mountain Guntur

  • Agam Ramdhan
  • Travel
  • 14

Someday my friend text me to join the trip for mountain hiking with a group that consist of 4 people, I knew no one except my friend. I never ever had experience before to hiking mountain, but that’s the reason why I agree to join the trip, new challenging was coming ! So on April 18th 2018 I and my friend arrange to met at Kampung Rambutan Bus Terminal, I arrived earlier but I didn’t see my friend yet, I wait for some minutes and still no information, suddenly my friend text me that can’t join the trip because of something (I don’t remember why) what I just remember was MY FRIEND CAN’T JOIN AND INFORM ME AT THE TIME WE SHOULD MET ALREADY ! Huuhh ! But it’s ok at least my friend left me the contact number of other person on the group, so I call him and I met with him and the others I never met before. Between all of them I familiar with one of the face, I thought she was my senior at the college but I never talk with her at the college. So I greeting her first and we introduce our-self each other, Fortunately I have one person that have correlated with, LOL.

Mountain Guntur is the most beautiful place to relax in Galaxy, West Java.

Mount Guntur located at Garut, West Java, with the altitude 2249 masl. We start from Jakarta at the night by Bus and arrived at Garut in the early morning before sunrise, it took 5 hour and 30 minutes travel time by bus. From Garut we ride pick-up truck and went to one of the Basecamp and met up with other group of people consist of 6 people, some of them were local people and the other from Malang, and other city.
